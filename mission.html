About ATKCO

AtkCo provides a holistic learning experience for professionals to stay ahead of the curve of emerging tech. We believe that by empowering the employee, we can maximize value for a company and further a community.

Mission

Build an organization that atrratcs, engages, and incentivizes our managing partners to be.

Vision

Atkco, Inc is a catalyst for workforce development in companies supporting product development from idea to product launch and beyond. Featuring our flagship training course, TBD.

With a 3 year strategic goal of generating $100,000,000 through human-centered training solutions, Atkco, Inc. seeks to implement an apprenticeship learning community that builds skills, networks, and creates staffing opportunities in tech for underserved communities.
