Sprint Planning How-To Guide

This guide explains key aspects of the Agile Sprint Planning at a team level. This document has the ability to answer basic questions about how to work in an Agile manner.
Step Description Guidelines
1. Understand capacity and velocity. Team assessess its capacity and expected velocity for the sprint, to understand what work it can commit to. *ScrumMaster helps the team review and enter capacity and set the target velocity for the sprint.
*Consider upcoming PTO, public holidays, trainings, appointments.
2. Set Sprint Goals Product Owner and team agree on what the goals of the spring should be.
Team sets weekly goals to drive progress towards sprint goals and balance work evenly across the sprint.		 *Goals should be specific and achievable.
*Goals should comprise targets for stories to be sequences throughout the sprint including story acceptance each week.
*Examples: "Complete customer profile update functionality; increase middleware unit test coverage to 50%.
